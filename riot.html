Valorant

◣──•~❉᯽❉~•──◢

Platform Played on: PC

Number of Months played: 8

Money spent: $80

Most Played Character: Killjoy

Most Played Gamemode: Competitive

Rating: 8.5/10

Reason: Valorant may be one of two games that I have a love-hate relationship with. Sometimes, my matches can go really well and I do amazing, other days I can go 0/10/2 (K/D/A). However, like many multiplayer games, I mainly play with my friens. They make those 10 loss streaks bareable and fun. Gameplay wise, Valorant is fun and unique with all its agents and their many abilities. With map glitches or lineups, a player has unlimited possibilities to win their match. There are many gamemodes that the game has to offer; if you get tired of normal gun shooting, you can switch to a rotating-weapons mode. Graphics wise, it's not bad, but there can be some improvements. Overall, Valorant isn't half bad when I'm winning with friends.

League of Legends

︼⋆︼⋆︼⋆︼⋆︼

Platform Played on: PC/Mobile (TFT)

Number of Months played: 12

Money spent: $0

Most Played Character: Ahri

Most Played Gamemode: Summoner's Rift: Normal

Rating: 7/10

Reason: League is not any better than Valorant, but I have significantly more play time on it due to my laptop's inability to run Valorant. Filled with toxic players, I often find myself being annoyed at their players and have to take breaks from playing the game. That's why I try to play with my friends rather than strangers. Graphics wise, the in-game graphics are as good as they get with a MOBA game, but their splash art is really nice. Each character comes with their own splash art that ties into their own lore. Some of them are interconnected in some way while others include interesting details. The characters also have cosmetics called "Skins". These skins allow players to look cool in-game (the rift) and offer lots of unique effects. Within skinlines, there are also lores of their own, thus making it a game perfect for lore fanatics like me. The game comes with many gamemodes like Norms (normal), Ranked, Flex (Ranked with 5 players of the same party), ARAM (All Mid All Random), and TFT (Teamfight Tactics).
